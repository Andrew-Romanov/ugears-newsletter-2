UGears STEM lab is a unique educational series for children and adults with a true passion for science
 
UGears STEM lab models are an intelligible and exciting way to learn about widely-used mechanisms, types of gearing and connections as well as their practical applications in real life using our AR app. Students learn key principles while engaging in practical tasks. This helps them not only get to the bottom of each scientific principle faster but also helps them develop other important skills that will later help them be more successful in life.
